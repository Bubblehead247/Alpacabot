## Project

Mean Reversion trading bot living in `MeansRev/`. See `MeansRev/SETUP.md` for setup and run instructions.

## Environment

Credentials are loaded from environment variables (`ALPACA_API_KEY`, `ALPACA_SECRET_KEY`). The `PAPER` flag in `MeansRev/config.py` controls paper vs. live trading.
